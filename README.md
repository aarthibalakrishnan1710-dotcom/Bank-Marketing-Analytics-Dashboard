🏦 Bank Marketing Analytics Dashboard

📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes a bank marketing dataset using Power BI to understand customer behavior, campaign performance, and term deposit subscriptions. The dashboard transforms raw banking data into meaningful business insights through interactive visualizations, KPI tracking, and customer segmentation. The objective is to help decision-makers identify trends, improve marketing strategies, and support data-driven decisions.

🎯 Business Objective

• Analyze customer demographics and campaign performance.

• Identify factors influencing term deposit subscriptions.

• Compare customer segments based on job, education, marital status, and account balance.

• Monitor overall campaign success using interactive dashboards.

• Provide actionable insights for improving future marketing campaigns.

📈 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

👥 Total Customers

💰 Successful Deposits

📈 Deposit Success Rate

💳 Average Account Balance

📊 Dashboard Features

✅ Customer Segmentation by Job

✅ Monthly Deposit Trend Analysis

✅ Education-wise Deposit Analysis

✅ Marital Status Distribution

✅ Average Account Balance Comparison

✅ Interactive Filters (Age Group, Job, Education, Month)

Dataset: Bank Marketing Dataset

Source: Kaggle

Records: 11,162 Customers

Industry: Banking & Marketing Analytics

📌 Business Insights

✔ Management professionals recorded the highest number of successful subscriptions.

✔ Secondary education customers contributed the largest share of successful deposits.

✔ Married customers represented the highest proportion of subscribers.

✔ Customers with higher average account balances showed better subscription rates.

✔ Monthly trend analysis identified periods with higher marketing campaign success.

✔ Interactive filtering enables quick analysis across different customer segments.
